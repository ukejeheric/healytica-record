Your Personal Health Management System

A secure and intuitive personal health management and electronic medical record (EMR) application. Healytica Record empowers users to own, organize, and manage their complete health history in one place.



Features

Core Features
- Dashboard - Real-time health overview and quick access to all features
- Vital Signs Tracking - Monitor blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen, temperature, weight, and more
- Lab Results Management - Store, organize, and share laboratory test results
- Medication Management - Track medications, dosages, schedules, and refills
- Complete Health Profile - Centralized personal health information and emergency contacts
- Security & Settings - HIPAA-compliant data management with full user control
- Data Privacy - Bank-level encryption, user-owned data, complete backup/export

Technical Features
- Fully Responsive - Perfect on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- Progressive Web App (PWA) - Works offline, installable on home screen
- Fast Performance - Optimized loading and seamless user experience
- Cloud-Based - Access your health data anywhere, anytime

Use Cases

For Individuals
- Track chronic disease management
- Monitor fitness and wellness goals
- Prepare for doctor appointments with complete history
- Share health information with family members
- Maintain emergency health information

For Healthcare Providers
- Access comprehensive patient health history
- Reduce manual record management
- Improve patient engagement
- Enable remote patient monitoring
- Streamline clinical workflows

For Clinics & Hospitals
- Integrate with existing EMR systems
- Improve patient care coordination
- Reduce duplicate testing
- Enhance patient communication
- Meet healthcare compliance requirements

## Tech Stack

### Frontend
- **HTML5** - Semantic markup and structure
- **CSS3** - Modern styling with responsive design
- **JavaScript (ES6+)** - Dynamic interactions and state management
- **Mobile Responsive** - Mobile-first design approach

### Backend & Authentication
- **Supabase** - Backend-as-a-Service (BaaS) platform
- **PostgreSQL** - Secure relational database
- **JWT Authentication** - Secure user authentication
- **Row-Level Security (RLS)** - User-level data protection

### Hosting & Deployment
- **Netlify** - Static site hosting and continuous deployment
- **GitHub** - Version control and CI/CD integration
- **Let's Encrypt** - Free HTTPS/SSL certificates

### Additional Technologies
- **Web Storage** - Local data caching
- **RESTful APIs** - Backend communication

## Security & Compliance

### Data Protection
- ✅ **AES-256 Encryption** - Industry-standard encryption
- ✅ **SSL/TLS** - Secure data transmission
- ✅ **User-Controlled Data** - Complete data ownership and access

### Authentication & Authorization
- ✅ **JWT Tokens** - Secure session management
- ✅ **Password Hashing** - Bcrypt encryption
- ✅ **Row-Level Security** - Database-level access control
- ✅ **Session Management** - Automatic timeout on inactivity

### Audit & Monitoring
- ✅ **Access Logs** - Track who accessed what data and when
- ✅ **Activity Audit Trail** - Complete history of all changes
